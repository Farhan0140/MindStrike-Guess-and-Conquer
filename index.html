Game Rules

Three Buttons

You have three buttons: Attack (to damage the enemy), Magical Shield (to defend yourself), and Heal (to recover your health).

Attack Button

Guess a number between 1 and 100. The closer your guess is to the correct number, the stronger your attack. The enemy's life decreases based on your attack power.

Magical Shield Button

Protects you from the enemy's attack. The enemy's attack damage reflects back on them, reducing their life instead.


You can use this button only 10 times

Heal Button

Guess a number between 1 and 100. The closer your guess is, the more health you recover.


You can use this button only 10 times
